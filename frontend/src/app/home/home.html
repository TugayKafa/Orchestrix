What is Orchestrix?

Priority-Based Task Scheduler with Dependency Resolution

Orchestrix is a task management system that understands dependencies between tasks. Instead of manually organizing execution order, simply define which tasks depend on what and assign their priorities.

The system automatically detects circular dependencies, offers retry for failed tasks, and keeps a full history of every execution. Whether you have 5 or 500 tasks, Orchestrix arranges them in the optimal order.
